Garbage

Selective recycling is applied; appropriate garbage is to be left in front of the main entrance of the building the evening before, after 20h.

!!! Organic (wet) waste must be placed outside with the bin.

  • Monday - organic (wet) waste
  • Tuesday - indifferentiated (dry)
  • Wednesday - organic (wet) waste & glass (separately)
  • Thursday - plastic & aluminium
  • Friday - organic (wet) waste
  • Saturday - paper and cardboard

On the day of departure, please leave the remaining sorted waste in the apartment.
