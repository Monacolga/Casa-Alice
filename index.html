At home

General information

  • The tap water is not potable
  • Check-in from 3 PM | Check-out by 11 AM
  • You can park the car in front of the building, or nearby if there’s no space available
  • Please respect the neighbors and keep noise to a minimum.
  • Before leaving, please close all windows and turn off all lights and air conditioners, leave the keys on the table
  • On the day of departure, please leave the remaining sorted waste in the apartment.
  • If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact ME, or in case of an urgent situation, my husband

    Garbage

    Selective recycling is implemented; please leave the appropriate garbage in front of the main entrance of the building the evening before collection, after 8 PM. Place organic (wet) waste outside in the designated bin.

    • Monday - organic (wet) waste
    • Tuesday - undifferentiated (dry)
    • Wednesday - organic (wet) waste & glass (separately)
    • Thursday - plastic & aluminium
    • Friday - organic (wet) waste
    • Saturday - paper and cardboard

    Funicular

    From Taormina to Mazzarò and back

    The funicular runs regularly every 15 minutes. In winter it operates every day from 8:00 to 20:00. In summer, however, the cable car is open until 1:30 at night.
